**VyBill** is a professional, high-speed offline billing software designed for retail shops, grocery stores, restaurants, cafes, and supermarkets in India. It turns any Windows PC or laptop into a powerful digital billing machine.

No internet required. No monthly fees. Fully offline, private, and secure.

---

## ⚡ Features (Free Offline Version)

*   **Fast Checkout:** Create and print bills in less than 5 seconds.
*   **GST & Non-GST Billing:** Configurable tax rates (GST, SGST, CGST) or simple tax-free invoices.
*   **Inventory & Stock Control:** Track stock levels, low-stock alerts, and item categories.
*   **Thermal Printer Support:** Instant printing for 2-inch and 3-inch thermal printers (or standard A4/A5 laser printers).
*   **Offline First:** All data is encrypted and saved locally on your computer. Your business data never leaves your device.
*   **Udhaar (Credit) Tracker:** Manage customer ledger records easily.
